Fonts:

Helvetica light

Georgia

Open Sans

The reason why I chose these fonts is because they are simple and sophisticated which is the look i am going for for this site. Open Sans is a very standard font and is not hard to read. I have made most of the headings big in size, and all text is presented in a fairly reasonable size font. Georgia is not so different from Open Sans either they are bothe legible and readable i made use of this font mainly to highlight some sub headings. And lastly Helvetica light which much like all the other fonts is also simple. All these fonts cater well to people who might have trouble with their eyesight as the fonts characters are easily identified.

My web pages barely make use of any colour, the dominating colour here is black and other colours are brought in through images made available as well as other places suchses headings and text which make use of different tints of white and black. The reason I opted for a minimalistic and simple colour palette is because i noticed that the content of ones page stands out more if it is colourful and against a plain background. And this is what I am aiming to achieve with my sight. I also like minimalistic colours because it moves one away from creating clutter and having too much colour on their sight which might prove to be disracting to the viewer. I also love the sophistication that comes with the colour black.
