As of right now i do not have much to say about my UX and UI because my webiste is at the beginning stages and alot can still change. As of right now my site is fairly simple to navigate making use of of little to no colour. My site consists mainly of black and white and a little splash of colour to make it stand out. the lack of colour gives it a sort of elegance and confidence which i feel fits the aesthetic I am going for because I am doing music I was aiming for a "Spotify" kind of look.

Looking at the legibility and readability of the site I would say is well done as i made us of fonts that are not hard to read the viewer can easily make out the characters of the word and the font is also of standard size, for people who might have eyesight problems. Text is mostly placed inside containers with a contratsing background and padding so that the text does not feel squashed making it hard to read.

Pages on the site make use of consistent visual weight mainly symmetrical and radial such as on my blog page where the contents are placed in the middle of the page. On the home page the albums are placed in straight rows and columns all of the same size.My site also makes use of thye smae flow as throughout the site is black with tints of white and splashes of colour from images
